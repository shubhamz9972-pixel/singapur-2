Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

We Have Answers

Everything you need to know before your visit to Singapur Restobar.

General

We are open every day — Monday through Sunday — from 12:00 PM to 2:00 AM. Kitchen service runs from 12 PM to 11:30 PM; the bar stays open till closing.
We are located in Model Town, Ludhiana, Punjab — 141002. You can find us on Google Maps by searching "Singapur Restobar Ludhiana". Valet parking is available on weekends.
Smart casual is our preferred style — especially for evenings and weekend DJ nights. We ask guests to avoid sports jerseys, flip-flops, and torn clothing during the night bar hours (after 8 PM). For lunch and early evening dining, you're welcome to dress comfortably.
Absolutely. During lunch and early evening dining hours (12 PM – 8 PM), we warmly welcome families and children. After 9 PM, the venue transitions to an adults-only nightlife atmosphere.

Reservations

Walk-ins are welcome, but we strongly recommend reserving a table for Friday and Saturday evenings, as we frequently reach full capacity. For private sections, DJ nights, or groups of 6 or more, a reservation is required.
For regular dining, 24–48 hours ahead is usually sufficient. For weekend evenings, DJ nights, or special occasions, we recommend booking 3–5 days in advance. Private event enquiries should be made at least 2 weeks before the intended date.
Yes. In the Additional Requests section of our reservation form, you can specify your preferences — such as a window table, bar-adjacent seating, a private corner, or a specific section. We do our best to accommodate, subject to availability.
We ask that you cancel or modify any reservation at least 4 hours before your booking time. For private event reservations, a 48-hour notice is required to avoid any cancellation fees.

Food & Drinks

Yes. Our menu features a dedicated selection of vegetarian and vegan dishes, including starters, mains, and desserts. Our kitchen is also happy to adapt several dishes upon request. Please inform your server of any dietary requirements when ordering.
Absolutely. Please inform us of any allergies when making your reservation and again when you arrive. Our team will liaise with the kitchen to ensure a safe and enjoyable meal. Common allergens (nuts, shellfish, gluten, dairy) are marked on our menus.
Yes. Our Chef's Journey Tasting Menu (6 courses, ₹3,500 per person) is available for pre-booking and features the week's finest ingredients with optional cocktail pairing. Please request it when making your reservation.

Private Events

Our venue can host up to 200 guests for a full-venue booking. We also offer semi-private sections for groups of 20–60, and intimate private dining rooms for 8–20 guests. Our events team will advise the best configuration for your occasion.
Yes. For private events, our chefs work with you to create a bespoke set menu tailored to your preferences, dietary requirements, and budget. Fixed-price packages start from ₹1,800 per person.
