Our Story

"We didn't just open a restaurant — we built a gateway to Singapore's soul, right here in Punjab."

Who We Are

Born of Passion

Singapur Restobar was born from a single trip to Singapore's hawker centres and hidden speakeasies. Our founders fell in love with the way Singapore blends Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Western flavours into something entirely its own — bold, refined, and unapologetically vibrant.

In 2024, they brought that magic to Ludhiana. Not as a replica, but as a reinterpretation — a place where the finest Pan-Asian cuisine meets Punjab's legendary hospitality, where craft cocktails flow under golden lights, and where every evening unfolds like a journey across Asian shores.

Today, Singapur Restobar is more than a dining destination. It's where Ludhiana comes to celebrate, connect, and indulge — seven nights a week.

Our Journey

Milestones

Early 2024

The Singapore Trip

Our founders visit Singapore and fall in love with its vibrant food culture — hawker centres by day, speakeasies by night. The dream of Singapur is born.

Mid 2024

Building the Vision

A heritage property in Model Town is transformed into an immersive dining experience — from dark-wood interiors to the custom-designed gold-lit bar.

Late 2024

Grand Opening

Singapur Restobar opens its doors. Within weeks, it becomes Ludhiana's most talked-about nightlife and dining destination.

2025

Expansion & Recognition

New seasonal menus, weekly live music, DJ residencies, and private event hosting make Singapur the city's go-to venue for every occasion.
